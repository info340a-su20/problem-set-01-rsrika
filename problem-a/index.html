Toph Beifong - Avatar: The Last Airbender

Toph is an earthbender from Avatar: The Last Airbender. She was born blind, so she has to rely on her earthbending and other senses in order to 'see'. She is very talented and does not let her disability limit her. She invented two other forms of bending, including metalbending and seismic sense, both of which were previously seen as impossible.

Reasons I admire Toph

  • She is sarcastic and funny
  • She is the Melon Lord
  • She gives people 'cool' nicknames
